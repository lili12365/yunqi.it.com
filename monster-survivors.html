About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an intense survival game where your skills and strategy determine how long you can last against increasingly difficult waves of monsters. Choose your character, upgrade your abilities, and fight to survive!

Game Features

• Multiple character classes with unique abilities
• Progressive difficulty system
• Various power-ups and upgrades
• Endless gameplay possibilities
• Stunning visual effects and animations

How to Play

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character. Automatically attack nearby enemies and collect experience orbs to level up. Choose new abilities and upgrades as you progress through the game. Survive as long as possible!

Tips & Strategies

• Keep moving to avoid being surrounded
• Prioritize defensive upgrades early in the game
• Collect experience orbs quickly to level up faster
• Watch out for elite monsters with special abilities
• Balance your character's offensive and defensive capabilities
