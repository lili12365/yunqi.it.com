About Tank Battle

Tank Battle is an intense multiplayer tank warfare game where you command your own tank in epic battles. Upgrade your tank, develop your strategy, and defeat your opponents to become the ultimate tank commander!

Game Features

• Multiple tank types with unique abilities
• Real-time multiplayer battles
• Extensive upgrade system
• Various battle maps
• Team-based gameplay

How to Play

Use WASD to move your tank and mouse to aim and shoot. Collect power-ups and upgrades during battle. Work with your team to defeat the enemy team. The last team standing wins!

Tips & Strategies

• Stay with your team for better coordination
• Use cover to avoid enemy fire
• Collect power-ups whenever possible
• Upgrade your tank's armor first
• Watch out for enemy snipers
